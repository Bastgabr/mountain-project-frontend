82 x 4000

This list tabulates all of the 82 official mountain summits of 4,000 metres (13,123 ft) or more in height in the Alps, as defined by the International Climbing and Mountaineering Federation (UIAA). They were selected primarily on a prominence of at least 30 metres (98 ft) above the highest adjacent col or pass.
